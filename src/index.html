From Crop to Customer

Whether you operate a farm or own a local food business, REKO software is designed to help you efficiently manage your business, market, and customers.

REKO empowers farmers and food vendors by simplifying and automating much of the management of their business processes.

We make it easy for customers to find and buy from you, We also help you manage inventory and orders, schedule and availability, orders across multiple locations, and more.

REKO was built with farmers helping design the software every step of the way

We are on a mission to help local farms thrive.

Quick Item Setup

Inventory Management

Order Management

Handle orders across multiple locations (farm stand, weekly drop locations, farmers markets, etc.) and all devices. Easily take orders in advance, manage the schedule and availability of items across multiple locations, and more.

What Will It Cost You?

Well, absolutely nothing!

REKO is 100% free for farmers and people selling food. All you need is a smartphone and an internet connection, and our platform will do the rest.

Reko manages the payment processing of all payments made using our software. When customers pay, we charge them a little bit extra (10%) that goes to cover the costs of payment processing (about 5%), leaving us with 5% to run the business.
